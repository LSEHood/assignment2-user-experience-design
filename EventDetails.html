Prydes EasiFeed Winter Show Hunter Accumulator

  • Points gained for each combination 1st = 6, 2nd = 4, 3rd = 3, 4th = 2, 5th = 1.
  • Must score over 65% to be eligible for points.
  • Riders must attend 2 or more days to qualify.
  • Prizes & Sashes to the top 6 hacks and top 6 ponies, covers for the winners

Rising Star Hack - Sash & Prize

  • Rising star hack cannot have competed in show hunter at Horse of Year or have more than 30 show hunter points in the previous season Aug 2020 - March 2021

Junior Rider - Sash & Prize

  • Any rider under the age of 14 not to have gained 30 registered points in the previous season Aug 2020 - March 2021

Winter Series Hi Points Pony & Hack

  • Points awarded to any horse & pony with points on a discipline leader board (top 20) points are awarded 20 - 1 = 1st - 20th
  • Horse must have points on two discipline leader boards to qualify. Horse/Pony may have different riders.
